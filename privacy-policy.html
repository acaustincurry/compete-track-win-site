Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Compete. Track. Win. ("CTW", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Compete mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo when you create an account via Apple Sign-In or email authentication.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Workout data from Apple HealthKit, including activity type, duration, distance, calories burned, and heart rate. This data is only accessed with your explicit permission and is used solely to calculate challenge scores.
  • Payment Information: Payment details are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers or bank account details on our servers.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, challenge participation history, and device information for improving our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate challenges, calculate scores, and determine winners
  • To process payments (buy-ins and payouts) through Stripe
  • To send important notifications about your challenges
  • To display your profile and rankings to other challenge participants
  • To improve and maintain our service

3. HealthKit Data

We access Apple HealthKit data only with your explicit authorization. HealthKit data is used exclusively for scoring challenges within the app. We do not sell, share, or use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes. HealthKit data is not stored on external servers beyond what is necessary to calculate and display challenge scores.

4. Payment Processing

All financial transactions are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We use Stripe Connect to facilitate payouts to challenge winners. We do not have direct access to your full payment card details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With other challenge participants (display name, profile photo, and challenge scores)
  • With Stripe for payment processing
  • With Firebase (Google) for app infrastructure and push notifications
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), secure Firebase authentication, and Stripe's PCI-compliant payment infrastructure to protect your information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Challenge history and scores are retained for record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through your device Settings. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. Participation in challenges involving monetary buy-ins requires users to be at least 18 years old.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email.
