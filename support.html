Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a challenge?

Tap "Join Challenge" on the home screen and enter the 6-character invite code provided by the challenge captain. You can also scan a QR code or tap a shared invite link.

How are workouts tracked?

The app syncs with Apple HealthKit to automatically pull your workout data. Make sure to grant HealthKit permissions when prompted. Open the app periodically to ensure your workouts sync.

How do payouts work?

When a challenge ends, winnings are automatically transferred to your connected bank account via Stripe. Set up your payout account in your Profile before a challenge ends to receive winnings promptly.

What if I didn't set up my payout account in time?

Don't worry — the app automatically retries payouts. Once you set up your Stripe Connect account in your Profile, your winnings will be sent automatically.

Can I get a refund?

If a challenge is cancelled before it starts, all buy-ins are automatically refunded. If you are removed from a challenge by the captain, your buy-in is refunded. For other refund requests, please contact us.

How do handicaps work?

In challenges with Captain Handicaps, the captain assigns a multiplier (0.25x to 1.5x) to each participant. Your raw workout points are multiplied by your handicap to calculate your adjusted score. A lower handicap means you earn fewer points per workout, leveling the playing field.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion, email us at leadership@compete-track-win.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and we will process your request within 30 days.
